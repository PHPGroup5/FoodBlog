Imperial Garden Seafood

Today We are going to introduce the review of Imperial Garden Rose Garden Hotel.

It offers two types of Menu, Al La Carte Buffet and A La Carte Menu. Al La Carte Buffet is available for Lunch and Dinner. For adult 16,000ks+ and for child 10,000ks+ . The time limit is 2 hours. Wastage Charges 3500ks for 200g.

For enquiry on birthday parties, company events, Imperial Garden Rose Garden Hotel is suitable. It is equipped with advanced audiovisual technology with 200 inch TV screen. This place can accommodate up to 250 people. We cater to birthday parties, company celebrations, charity events, product launches and other gatherings can all be accommodated with planning, catering, venue decoration, entertainments. Services:
1) Seated Dinner or Buffet Set-Up
2) Venue Decorations
3) Event Entertainment
4) Dessert Table Set up

This is about chinese food Restaurant so it is a best place for chinese food lover. Dimsum, seafood, Jellyfish salad, honey roast pork, Spicy boiled fish, braised chicken can be ordered. We can taste the chef's delious dishes warmly.

There maybe a lot of customers so the dishes will last longer than we thought. If possible, please phone to book reservation.

For restaurant address: 171 Upper Pansoden Road yangon.
